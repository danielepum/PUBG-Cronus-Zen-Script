⚙️ **How Andry 85 PUBG V6 Works**

The script revolves around three independent gameplay profiles: Primary Weapon, Secondary Weapon, and Looting.

Players configure each weapon individually through the OLED menu, assigning separate recoil values, scope corrections, and stance adjustments.

Whenever ADS and Fire are held simultaneously, the progressive anti-recoil engine activates, applying different recoil corrections during the start, middle, and end of sustained fire.

The stance tracking system automatically detects whether the player is standing, crouching, or prone, then applies the appropriate recoil modifier.

Scope corrections are added on top of the base recoil profile, allowing every optic to receive its own independent tuning.

Additional combat mods such as Rapid Fire, Burst Fire, Peek Shot, Strafe, Hold Breath, and Jump Shot activate only when enabled through the OLED menu.

Players can instantly switch between Primary and Secondary weapon profiles while the Looting profile temporarily disables combat modifications during inventory management.

All configured values are saved permanently through the integrated PVAR save system.
